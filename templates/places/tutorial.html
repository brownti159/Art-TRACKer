Art TRACKer is an interactive guide to explore public art in Durham, NC.


Each circle on the map represents a public work of art that you can visit: murals, sculptures, and painted crosswalks.


Tap on a circle to see a pop-up preview of the artwork.


Tap "See Details" on the pop-up to learn more about that work of art, including a description of the work, artist information, and wheelchair accessibility.


Use the map locations to explore a few or many art installations around Durham.
